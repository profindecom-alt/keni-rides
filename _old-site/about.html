Our Mission

Reliable transport, built for Moroccans.

We believe everyone deserves a safe, comfortable ride — whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or exploring a new city. Our platform connects riders with vetted drivers through technology that's fast, transparent, and fair.

No hidden charges. No surge pricing. Just honest fares and drivers who take pride in their work.

Our Values

The principles we drive by.

Safety First

Every driver undergoes criminal background checks, vehicle safety inspections, and professional training before joining the platform.

Radical Transparency

You see the full fare before you book, the driver's name and rating, and live tracking throughout. No surprises — ever.

Community Impact

We create economic opportunity for drivers and their families, while reducing congestion and emissions in cities through shared mobility.

Constant Innovation

Our engineering team works every day to improve matching algorithms, reduce wait times, and build tools that make both riders' and drivers' lives easier.

Fair Pricing

Affordable for riders, fair for drivers. We structure our commission model to ensure drivers earn a living wage while keeping fares competitive.

Local First

Built in Morocco, for Morocco. We understand the local context — traffic patterns, city nuances, and cultural expectations — better than any global competitor.
