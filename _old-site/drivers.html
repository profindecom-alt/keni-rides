How It Works

Start driving in 3 days.

1. Sign up online
Fill in the form below. Takes 5 minutes. We'll review your application within 24 hours.
2. Submit documents
Upload your driver's license, vehicle registration, and ID. We run a background check — usually done within 48 hours.
3. Complete orientation
A short online training module covering the Keni app, safety protocols, and how to get great ratings.
4. Start earning!
Your driver app is activated and you can start accepting ride requests immediately. First week earnings include a 200 MAD welcome bonus.

Driver Requirements

At least 21 years old
Valid Moroccan driver's license (2+ years)
Clean driving record
Pass background check
Smartphone (Android 9+ or iOS 14+)

Vehicle Requirements

Model year 2016 or newer
4-door vehicle (sedan, SUV, or MPV)
Valid vehicle registration (Carte Grise)
Valid vehicle insurance
Pass Keni vehicle inspection
