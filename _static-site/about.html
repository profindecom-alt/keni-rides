Mohamed “Simo” Bennis — Founder

The Founder

Born to Ride Morocco

Keni Rides was founded by Mohamed (Simo) Bennis, a passionate motorcycle rider and explorer. Since childhood he has travelled across Morocco, discovering every region of the country — every pass, every piste, every hidden village café worth stopping at.

From the golden dunes of the Sahara Desert to the snow-capped peaks of the Atlas Mountains, from the turquoise coves of the Mediterranean to the wild surf of the Atlantic Coast — Simo has ridden it all, in every season.

His dream became a company: a place where travelers experience Morocco not from a tour-bus window, but from the saddle of a great motorcycle — with the freedom to explore and the backup of someone who knows every kilometre.
