Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you ride. Still unsure? Ask us directly.

How do I book a motorcycle?

Choose your motorcycle and your pickup & return dates, then send the booking form on the bike's page. Our team contacts you shortly to confirm availability. A 20% deposit secures your reservation and the balance is paid at pickup — no online payment is required.

Can I ride anywhere in Morocco?

Yes — from the Sahara to the Atlas and both coasts, the country is yours. We simply ask you to share your planned route so we can advise on road conditions, fuel stops and seasons, and make sure assistance can reach you anywhere.

What documents are required?

Four things: a motorcycle licence issued more than 2 years ago, your passport or national ID, a hotel address or proof of accommodation, and a credit card under the renter's name. Minimum age is 21. Details on the rental conditions page.

Is insurance included?

Every motorcycle includes third-party liability insurance. In addition, personal travel insurance that covers motorcycle riding is mandatory — we may ask for proof before the rental begins.

Can motorcycles be delivered?

Yes. Marrakech / Casablanca: €50 delivery, €50 pickup. Tangier / Fez: €100 delivery, €100 pickup. Other destinations are quoted individually. Available Monday–Sunday, by appointment, with 2 days notice.

Is riding equipment included?

Helmets, gloves, jackets and waterproof suits are available as optional extras. Note that a helmet and gloves are mandatory on every rental — bring your own or add ours.

What happens in case of breakdown?

Call us — any day, any hour. If the motorcycle can't be repaired quickly, we provide an equivalent motorcycle, an alternative solution, or a rental discount if necessary. Your adventure keeps rolling.

Can beginners rent a motorcycle?

Yes — if you're 21 or older and have held your motorcycle licence for more than 2 years. For newer off-road riders we recommend the friendly Suzuki DR200 or Honda CRF250, and we're happy to suggest gentle routes.

What payment methods are accepted?

The 20% reservation deposit and the balance can be paid by credit card or cash. The security deposit (€500–€2000 depending on the motorcycle) is authorized on your card but never charged unless there is damage.

Read the fine print — it's short and fair.
