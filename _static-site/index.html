The Story

The Adventure at the Heart of Keni Rides

Keni Rides was founded by Mohamed (Simo) Bennis — a passionate rider who has explored every region of Morocco since childhood. His dream: to let travelers experience the real Morocco through authentic motorcycle adventures.

  • A lifetime on Moroccan roads — from Sahara pistes to Rif coast switchbacks.
  • Rider-to-rider service — honest advice on bikes, routes and seasons.
  • Authentic experiences — the Morocco tourists never see.
